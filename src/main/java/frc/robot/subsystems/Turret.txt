Turret (Subsystem)
    The turret acts as a way of aiming the shooter (which makes it easier for the driver). 
    This enables shooting while intaking, more precise shooting in general.

Functions:
- stop()
    Stops the turret motors.

- calculateTargetAngle(Rotation2d angle)
    Takes the shot angle from the robot to the target, and then adjusts this angle for the turret.

- moveToAngle(double angle)
    Takes an angle and moves the turret to face that angle.

- fixTurretToAngle(double angle)
    Takes an angle and moves the turret to face that angle. The turret will stop tracking the target until it is told to again.

- startTrackingTarget()
    Makes the turret begin targeting an angle. This will also apply if the turret has been fixed to an angle.

- periodic()
    Logs turret angle, target angle. Also grabs shot information, and readjusts the target angle.
