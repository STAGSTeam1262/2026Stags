Shooter (Subsystem)
    The shooter shoots fuel into the hub and onto our alliance side when passing.
    ShotData is sent from the superstructure, which is then used to calculate shooter speed.

Functions:
- stop()
    Stops all motors.

- setShooterVoltage(double voltage)
    Sets the voltage of the shooter.

- calculateShooterVoltage(double distance)
    Uses the distance from a target (in meters) to
    calculate the speed the shooter should be running at.

- periodic()
    Logs shooter voltage and other random information.
